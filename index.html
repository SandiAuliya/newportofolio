Hi!

Azmi Askandar, when you hear this name, you immediately remember how melodious the sholawat and 16-year-old man recited. Joining the Syubbanul Muslimin prayer group, this handsome man has managed to make a name for himself and is loved by fans of all ages. Until now, Azmi is still a student at the Nurul Qodim Islamic Boarding School, Kalijakar, Probolinggo. It was here that he joined the Syubbanul Muslimin prayer group and made this group more known to the public. Some of the titles of religious songs and prayers that Azmi beautifully performed include Yes Asyiqal Mustafa, Love in Istikharah, My Mother I Miss, Al Fatihah For You, Annabi Shollu Alaih, to Ya Rosulullah Version of a Cup of Honey
